Dish-Ingredients

Dish1:mutton Curry

  1. Onion: 1 large, chopped
  2. Tomato: 2 large, pureed
  3. Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tablespoon
  5. Bay leaf: 1
  6. Cumin seeds: 1 teaspoon
  7. Coriander powder: 1 tablespoon
  8. Turmeric powder: 1 teaspoon
  9. Red chili powder: 1 teaspoon (adjust to taste)
  10. Garam masala: 1 teaspoon
  11. Salt: to taste
  12. Oil: 3 tablespoons
  13. Coconut milk (optional): 1 cup
  14. Fresh coriander leaves: for garnish

Dish2:egg Curry

  • egg - 250g (cut into cubes)
  • Onion - 1 large (chopped)
  • Tomato - 2 large (pureed)
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 2 tbsp
  • Green chilies - 2-3 (slit)
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1 tsp (adjust to taste)
  • Coriander powder - 1 tsp
  • Cumin powder - 1 tsp
  • Garam masala - 1 tsp
  • Kashmiri chili powder - 1/2 tsp (for color)
  • Yogurt - 1/2 cup
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves) - 1 tsp
  • Coriander leaves - for garnish
  • Oil - 2-3 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste

Dish3:Egg noodles

  1. 2 large eggs, beaten
  2. 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  3. 1/2 cup chopped onion
  4. 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  5. 1/2 cup chopped green peas
  6. 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  7. 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  8. 1 teaspoon oyster sauce (optional)
  9. Salt to taste
